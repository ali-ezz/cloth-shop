# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We expect all contributors to be respectful, professional, and supportive. We are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive community.

## Our Standards

- Be considerate and kind.
- Use welcoming language.
- Assume good intentions.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Respect differing viewpoints.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the repository maintainers.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who do not follow the code of conduct.
